About Me.

I'm a cybersecurity learner with a growing passion for ethical hacking, digital privacy, and uncovering how systems can break and how to defend them better.
Whether it’s dissecting vulnerabilities, exploring real-world attack vectors, or simulating threats in controlled environments, I thrive in spaces where curiosity meets discipline. I’m currently diving into penetration testing, red teaming, OSINT, and threat detection with a strong focus on practical, hands-on learning.
My goal isn’t just to break things it’s to understand them deeply, communicate findings clearly, and contribute to a more secure digital world. This portfolio showcases my journey, my projects, and my evolving perspective on cybersecurity.
